# Title of Specification Update Proposal(SUP)
## Owner
> Name of the person who will be driving the SUP to completion

## Summary
> Summary (in layman's terms) explaining the purpose of the SUP

## Reason for proposal
> Indication of how it improves on what we have in the specification and why they feel this SUP is needed (if there isn't a pre-existing feature)

## Requirements alignment acknowledgement
> Acknowledgement that the SUP meets minimum requirements and doesn't introduce any requirements that are out of Margo's scope or vision.

### Associated Feature Link(s)
> Provide links to applicable feature(s) and a statement about any requirements that were agreed to be out of scope for the SUP.  

## Technical Proposal
> The SUP's technical details. Ensure there is enough technical details to enable someone to implement the proposal on their own.

## Alternatives Considered (optional)
> List any alternative solutions considered while working on the SUP and the reason for not choosing them. If the SUP owner knows that there is a risk of a competing SUP, they can use this section to make their case ahead of any potential votes on why their solution is better for the specification.
